# Weekly Architecture Review

## Purpose
Keep the system alive and coherent.

## Weekly checklist
- What new outputs were created?
- Were they routed to the correct home?
- Were any outputs left floating?
- Did any tool roles drift?
- Are any secrets or access records outdated?
- Are next actions defined?
- What needs promotion, demotion, or repair?

## Plain-language rule
Once a week, check that the system still makes sense.

## Quality law
Correct. Coherent. Clear. Precise.
