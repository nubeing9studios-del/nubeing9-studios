# Monthly Sovereignty Review

## Purpose
Review movement toward stronger control, stability, ownership, and technical sovereignty.

## Monthly questions
- What still depends too heavily on external tools?
- What can now be centralized through OpenRouter?
- What can now be localized through Ollama?
- What should be preserved in GitHub?
- What can OpenClaw eventually operationalize?
- What paid tools are still worth keeping for speed?
- What open or self-hosted alternatives are now viable?

## Plain-language rule
Once a month, ask whether the system is becoming more owned, more stable, and more yours.

## Quality law
Correct. Coherent. Clear. Precise.
